An introduction to Drawing!

Art! There are many different ways to make art today. Art has shifted throughout the course of time from photo realistic paintings, to real photos, to abstract. Art is a way for people to convey emotions and spread awareness. A way to tell a story or to just make pretty images. There are many different movements in the art world but this isn't an art history website. Here you can learn about drawing techniques, mediums, and digital platforms. There are many different forms of drawing in todays world, even just creating art has a whole new meaning. Many people draw traditionally and others draw for their hobbies or freetime. Some great apps to go see others' artwork is YouTube, Deviantart, Pinterest, Instagram, and Tumblr.
