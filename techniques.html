Drawing Techniques

Want to start drawing? Here are some basic techniques to get started.

    Hatching:

    This technique involves creating parallel lines of varying length and weight on paper to create shading, texture and contrast.

    Line Wieght:

    technique involves drawing lines close or farther apart with preassure on the pen or pencil.

    Dots:

    Technique for creating texture and shading by dorming dots on the paper. Creates a gradation and stipple texture.

    Blending:

    A technique used to create seamless gradation of values. It involves smudging the surface with a blending tool or fingertips.

    Contour:

    A technique that involves using continous line to capture edges of objects. Useful for drawing objects with defined shapes, such as still life or landscpes.
    Countouring uses broad strokes to create shadows and highlights. Strokes are closer together for shadows and further apart for highlights.
